About SketchWeaver

SketchWeaver is an innovative AI-powered sketch generation app with a warm apricot color theme that creates a cozy and inspiring creative environment. Instantly turn your photos into artistic sketches with unique personality and detail.

Core Features

One-Click Conversion

Upload your photo and let AI automatically convert it into a refined sketch in seconds.

Smart AI Detection

Automatically detects edges and lines, optimizing the sketch effect without manual adjustments.

Detail Preservation

Keeps the original details intact while adding a natural hand-drawn texture.

Personal Portfolio

All creations are automatically saved, allowing you to manage and revisit your artworks anytime.

Favorites & Tags

Mark your favorite pieces and organize them with custom tags for easy browsing.

Before & After Comparison

Swipe to compare original photos with sketches and explore the AI transformation process.

Easy Sharing

Share your creations directly to your favorite social platforms with ease.
